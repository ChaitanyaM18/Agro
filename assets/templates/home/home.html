About Us

Agriculture is very much essential for the maintenance of our life. But regrettably it is largely unorganized, the assured quality seeds which is critical for attaining high crop yields are out of reach due to high prices. Govt. programs proposed for the benefit of farming do not reach small farmers.

The aim of the project is to help the farmers for their betterment and obtain greater profit by having direct farmer to supplier/dealer communications. This project is constructive because farmer can directly communicate with retailers and suppliers without taking help of any third parties. Our aim is to create awareness, boost business communication and transparency in the system by having direct farmer to supplier and dealer communication. Also it does notify the farmer via SMS regarding any advertisement or offer by dealer.

Separate Login Separate login areas with appropriated functionality for buyers / farmers, administrations and dealers / retailers.

Communication Between buyer and Seller This innovative site allow a communication between buyer and seller for suitable product to enhance the productivity and quality of crops. It allows the buyers to login and communicate to respective dealer.

Complaints A separate page where farmers / buyer can post complaints and only assigned administrators can receive and read the complaint.
